### Usage
- **Behavioural tendencies** inform the simulation logic: e.g., a Gunslinger QB increases the probability of deep throws and turnovers, while a Bruiser RB calls more inside runs.
- **Relationship & morale effects** modify the cohesion model: diva tendencies lower trust, locker leaders raise it, and role models improve development of younger players.
- **Scheme fit** (not shown in table) should link each label to offensive or defensive playbooks (e.g., SlotReceivers thrive in West Coast and Spread systems, BullRushers excel in 3‑4 defense).  This will be mapped when refining playbook modelling.
